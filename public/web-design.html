Do I need to write any content myself?

No. On Standard and Managed I write everything. You fill in a short brief about your business and I take it from there. Most people are surprised how accurately it comes out.

What if I don't like the design?

Before I write any code you can change as much as you like. Once it's built, Standard has one round of changes and Managed has two. In practice most people are happy first time.

Will I be able to update it myself?

Yes. I record a short video walkthrough of your actual site so you can update text, swap images, and add pages without calling me.

Do you offer payment plans?

Half upfront, half when it goes live. That's it. No monthly fees unless you're on the Managed package.
